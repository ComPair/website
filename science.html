AMEGO will provide unprecedented advances in three areas of MeV astrophysics, 1) time domain astrophysics in the MeV regime, probing particle acceleration in a broad range of galactic and extragalactic objects, 2) deepest view of the entire MeV sky addressing the many new questions raised by Fermi. This is particularly relevant in the MeV energy band where most Fermi sources emit their peak power, 3) exploration of the nuclear line universe, probing the creation of key elements created in explosive and dynamic astrophysical environments.
